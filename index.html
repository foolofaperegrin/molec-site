MOLEC

A modular creature-building roguelite set in a strange pelagic world of drifting synthetic life.

Begin as a small larval organism, gather parts and resources, and piece-by-piece grow into something larger, stranger, and more capable.

In Molec, creatures are assembled from modular parts that can be chained together into limbs, tools, armour, and weapons. Different configurations create different movement styles, abilities, and survival strategies.
