MOLEC

A modular creature-building roguelike set in a strange mechanical plankton ecosystem.

Begin life as a tiny larval organism drifting in the pelagic. Collect parts, evolve your creature, and grow into a powerful mechanical beast.

Build Your Creature

In MOLEC, creatures are assembled from modular parts that chain together to form limbs and structures. Different combinations produce unique movement styles, abilities, and combat strategies.

  • ⚙️ Modular parts with unique motion and abilities
  • 🧬 Emergent movement from physical limb chains
  • 🦑 Grow from a fragile larva to a complex organism
  • 🔁 Endless build combinations every run

Game Pillars

  • Creation — design strange mechanical organisms
  • Discovery — uncover new parts and behaviours
  • Problem Solving — adapt builds to survive hazards and predators
  • Power — grow into an intricate apex creature
