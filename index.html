MOLEC

Build strange mechanical plankton and grow from a fragile larva into a powerful pelagic organism.

MOLEC is a modular creature-building roguelike set in a strange marine ecosystem of drifting mechanical life.

Chain together moving parts, adapt your build, gather resources, and survive predators and hazards as your creature evolves into something larger, stranger, and more capable.

Follow development Learn more
  • Core fantasy Grow your own weird mechanical beastie
  • Key hook Emergent movement from chained, animated parts
  • Touchstones Snake, Spore, Lego

What it is

  • Build your creature
    Assemble modular parts into long chains, limbs, armour, tools, and weapons.
  • Discover new combinations
    Collected parts and upgrades create surprising synergies across every run.
  • Adapt to survive
    Change your build to deal with predators, hazards, resource scarcity, and combat.
  • Grow into power
    Start tiny and vulnerable, then become a larger and more intricate organism.

Game pillars

  • Creation — design unusual creatures from modular parts.
  • Discovery — uncover new parts, behaviours, and upgrade combinations.
  • Problem Solving — tune your build to meet environmental and combat challenges.
  • Power — become more complex, capable, and dangerous over the course of a run.
