MOLEC

A modular creature-building roguelike set in a strange pelagic world of drifting mechanical life.

Begin as a small larval organism, gather parts and resources, and slowly grow into something larger, stranger, and more capable.

In MOLEC, creatures are assembled from modular parts that can be chained together into limbs, tools, armour, and weapons. Different configurations create different movement styles, abilities, and survival strategies.

What shapes the game

Build your beastie

Reconfigure collected parts into new forms at any time.

Emergent movement

Motion comes from chained parts interacting together.
